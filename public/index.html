Game Controls & Mechanics

1. Push Button

Tap to push the ball forward. The more power you have, the farther it goes!

2. Charge Button

Hold to charge up your strength. Charging will:

  • Increase your push power
  • Grow your ball in size

⚠️ Bigger balls are heavier and harder to push, but they hit harder!

3. Boost Button

Activate to multiply your push power!

  • Press manually at any time
  • Or it activates automatically when you're about to lose, as long as you have enough Boost Energy

⚡ Boost Energy Tips

The bigger your ball, the less Boost Energy it can hold

Use your Boost wisely!
